About Cannonballs

Cannonballs is an addictive arcade game that puts your aiming skills and reflexes to the test. Launch cannonballs to destroy targets, solve puzzles, and advance through increasingly challenging levels.

With vibrant graphics and intuitive controls, Cannonballs offers an engaging gaming experience for players of all ages. The game features realistic physics that make each shot feel satisfying and authentic.

Whether you're looking for a quick gaming session during your break or want to challenge yourself with complex levels, Cannonballs provides hours of entertainment right in your browser.

Game Features

  • Multiple exciting levels with increasing difficulty
  • Realistic physics engine for authentic cannon shooting
  • Challenging obstacles and puzzles to overcome
  • Smooth, responsive controls for precise aiming
  • Vibrant graphics and engaging sound effects

How to Play

  1. 1

    Aim Your Cannon

    Use your mouse or touch screen to aim the cannon in the direction you want to shoot.

  2. 2

    Adjust Power

    Click and hold to adjust the power of your shot - the longer you hold, the more powerful the shot will be.

  3. 3

    Release to Fire

    Release to fire your cannonball and watch it follow a realistic trajectory.

  4. 4

    Hit Targets

    Destroy all targets in each level to progress to the next challenge.

  5. 5

    Watch Your Ammo

    Some levels have limited ammunition, so plan your shots carefully!
